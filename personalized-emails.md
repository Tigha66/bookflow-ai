## Quick Send Formula

1. Subject: [Benefit or Result]
2. Hook: 1 sentence on their pain point
3. 3 bullet points on what you do
4. Call to action: "Interested in a demo?"
5. Sign off

Keep emails short — 5-6 sentences max.
